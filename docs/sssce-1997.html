DELTA INTELLECT

SSSCE 1997

Question 1:

The sets \(A=\{..., -6,-4,-2,0,2,4,6,...\}, B=\{ 0 \leq x \leq 9 \}\) and \(C=\{x:-4 < x \leq 0 \}\) are subsets of \(\mathbb{Z}\), the set of integers.

    1. Describe the members of the set \(A^\prime\), where \(A^\prime\) is the compliment of \(A\).
    2. Find \(A^\prime \cap B\).
  2. Represent the sets \(B\) and \(C\) on a Venn diagram.

Question 2:

Without using calculators or tables

  1. simplify \(\left( 1\displaystyle\frac{2}{7} - \displaystyle\frac{1}{3} \right)\times 1 \displaystyle\frac{3}{4}\);
  2. evaluate \(\displaystyle\frac{7.25 \times (0.16)^2}{0.004}\)

Question 3:

\(P^\prime (8, -2)\) is the image of the point \(P(5,2)\) by the translation vector \(\textbf{v}\). Find

  1. the vector \(\textbf{v}\);
  2. the coordinates of the point \(Q\) which maps onto the point \(Q^\prime(5, -2)\) under \(\textbf{v}\).
  3. \(\overrightarrow{P^\prime Q^\prime}\);
  4. \(|P^\prime Q^\prime|\).

Question 4:

If \(3^m \times 3^n = 243\) and \(3^m \div 3^{2n}=9\), write down two equations connecting \(m\) and \(n\). Hence, find the values of \(m\) and \(n\).

Question 5:

Using ruler and a pair pair of compasses only, construct a triangle \(ABC\) in which angle \(A=45^\circ, |AB|=7\) cm and \(|AC|=9\) cm. Locate a point \(P\), inside the triangle \(ABC\), 5 cm from \(A\) and equidistant from \(B\) and \(C\). Find \(|PB|\).

Question 6:

In the figure, \(TB\) touches the circle at \(B\) and \(BD\) is a diameter. \(\angle TBA=31^\circ\) and \(\angle BAC=69^\circ\). Calculate i. \(\angle ADC\) ii. \(\angle ABC\) iii. \(\angle CAD\)

  1. In the triangle \(ABC, |AB|=5\) cm, \(|BC|=8\) cm and \(|AC|=6\) cm. \(P\) is a point on \(AB\) such that \(|AP|=2\) cm. The line through \(P\) parallel to \(BC\) meets \(AC\) at \(Q\). Calculate
    1. \(|PQ|\);
    2. \(|QC|\).

Question 7:

  1. The cost of a packet of sugar is \(x\) cedis and the cost of of a tin of milk is \(y\) cedis. If 3 packets of sugar and 4 tins of milk cost 635.00 cedis and 4 packets of sugar and 3 tins of milk cost 695.00 cedis, write two equations connecting \(x\) and \(y\). Hence, find \(x\) and \(y\).

  2. The cost of production of a wireless set was 40,000.00. The manufacturer sold it to a wholesaler at a profit of 20 percent. the wholesaler also sold it at a profit of 25 percent. The retailer marked the set to be sold at a price 50 percent above what he paid for it.
    1. Find the marked price
    2. To a customer who paid cash, the retailer reduced the price to 75,000.00 cedis. Find the percentage discount.

Question 8:

  1. Without using tables or calculator, evaluate \(3\sqrt{7}(7-2\sqrt{7})\), if \(\sqrt{7}=2.646\).

  2. The cost or producing a motor car in a certain factory is partly constant and partly varies inversely as a number of cars produced per day. If the cost of producing each car is 1,600 dollars when 4 cars are produced per day and 1,420 dollars when 5 cars are produced per day, find the rate of production necessary to bring the cost down to 1,150 dollars per car.

Question 9:

The table below shows the heights, measured to the nearest metre, of 300 trees.

Height(m) 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9
No. of trees 14 21 42 83 118 12 7 3
  1. Draw a histogram to represent the data.
  2. Calculate the mean of the distribution, correct to the nearest metre.
  3. If a tree is selected at random, find the probability that is is at least 6 metres tall.

Question 10:

  1. Copy and complete the following table of values for the relation \(y=\displaystyle\frac{2x(x-1)}{x+3}\), for the interval \(-1 \leq x \leq 6.\)
\(x\) \(-1\) \(0\) \(0.5\) \(1\) \(2\) \(3\) \(4\) \(5\) \(6\)
\(2x(x-1)\) \(0\) \(-0.5\) \(0\) \(4\) \(24\) \(40\) \(60\)
\(x+3\) \(2\) \(3.5\) \(3\) \(7\) \(9\)
\(y\) \(-0.14\) \(0\) \(3.43\) \(6.67\)

  1. Using a scale of 2 cm to 1 unit on both axes, draw the graph of \(y=\displaystyle\frac{2x(x-1)}{x+3}\) for th given interval.

  2. Use your graph to
    1. find the range of values of \(x\) for which \(y\) is negative.
    2. estimate, correct to one decimal place, the value of \(x\) for which \(2x(x-1)=x+3\).

Question 11:

  1. Using a scale of 2 cm to 2 units on both axes, draw on a graph sheet two perpendicular axes \(Ox\) and \(Oy\) for \(-10 \leq x \leq 10\) and \(-10 \leq y \leq 10\).

  2. Draw, labeling all the vertices clearly together with their coordinates,
    1. triangle \(ABC\) with vertices \(A(4, 8), B(1, 7)\) and \(C(3, 4)\).
    2. the image \(\bigtriangleup A_1 B_1 C_1\) of \(\bigtriangleup ABC\) under a reflection in the line \(y=3\), where $A A_1, B B_1 $ and \(C \to C_1\).
    3. the image \(\bigtriangleup A_2 B_2 C_2\) of \(\bigtriangleup ABC\) under an anticlockwise rotation through \(90^\circ\) about the origin, where $A A_2, B B_2 $ and \(C \to C_2\).
    4. the image \(\bigtriangleup A_3 B_3 C_3\) of \(\bigtriangleup ABC\) under an enlargement with scale factor \(-1\) from the origin, where $A A_3, B B_3 $ and \(C \to C_3\).
    5. What single transformation maps \(\bigtriangleup A_2 B_2 C_2\) onto \(\bigtriangleup A_3 B_3 C_3\).

Question 12:

The model below shows the pyramid \(EFGHV\) on a cuboid \(ABCDEFGH. |AB|=6\) cm, \(|BC|=6\) cm and \(|CG|=2\) cm.

The volume of the model is 132 cm\(^3\). Find, correct to the nearest whole number,

  1. the height of the pyramid
  2. the length of the slant edge, \(VG\);
  3. the angle between the face \(VFG\) and the base \(EFGH\). [Take \(\pi\) = 3.142]
